What Are Gene-based Diets?

Ever wonder why a diet that works wonders for your friend doesn’t do the same for you? The answer lies in your genes. Your DNA shapes how you process the nutrients from your food, while the food you eat can actually influence how those genes behave.

Traditional diet plans look at the basics - your weight, test reports, health history, and food habits. Personalised, yes, but not enough. Gene-based diet plans add the missing piece: your genetic blueprint. For instance, some people easily convert beta-carotene from vegetables into active vitamin A, while others can’t do it as efficiently - meaning they may need different food sources to meet the same requirement.

This combines nutrition science with your genetics, creating a plan that works ‘with’ your body, not ‘against’ it, making your health journey smarter, easier, and more effective.
