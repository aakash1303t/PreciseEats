About Precise Eats

Precise Eats is your partner in creating a healthier lifestyle that truly works for you. As an online diet clinic, it offers practical, customized nutrition plans designed to simplify healthy eating and fit naturally into your daily routine. The focus is on making wellness both easy and enjoyable - proving that small, everyday changes can lead to powerful, long-term results.

At Precise Eats, food isn’t about restriction; it’s about balance, flavor, and nourishment. With trusted guidance and thoughtfully tailored plans, Precise Eats helps you take charge of your health and enjoy the journey toward feeling your very best.

About the Diet Coach

Krishnaveni is a Nutrition Practitioner, Nutrigenomics Specialist, and Certified Diabetes Educator who loves helping people build a healthier relationship with food and with themselves. She believes lasting change comes from within, and that consistency matters more than chasing numbers or strict targets.

For her, true wellness is about how you feel - energized, content, and confident - not just what the scale says. With a gentle, supportive approach, Krishnaveni helps her clients make small, meaningful changes, enjoy their meals without guilt, and create a lifestyle that feels natural, joyful, and sustainable.
